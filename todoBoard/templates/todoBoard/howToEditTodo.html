HOW TO EDIT TO-DO

1. Click 'TO-DOS' in the upper left corner.
2. On the ToDo overview find the entry you want to edit.
3. Click the blue "edit" button next to the entry.
4.Make your changes: new description / completion / due
5. Click the green "Submit" button.
6. Proof your changes.
